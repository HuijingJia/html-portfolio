The Best Movies According to Kate Jia

My top 3 movies of all-time

No.1 Interstellar

Released in October 26, 2014. Directed by Christopher Nolan.
I have watched it more than six times. I like the pictures and the pace of the movie.
The story is complex and I feel like collecting up some different ideas each time after I watched it!
It shows the beauty of the universe and the music is fatastic!

No.2 Dunkirk

I can feel the fear in front of the screen.
I hate war, and thanks the movie so that I can realize how cruel the war is instead of experiencing it by real.
As a word saying "There are no good wars and neither no bad peace!"

NO.3 Forrest Gump

I really like the story!
I love all the characters in the movie.
Each time after I watched the movie, the character "Forest Gum" gave me strength,
I can see the hope of life, and I can find some inner peace.
It enables me to fight for my dreams and be a good person.
